Ada Lovelace wrote the first "software" programs before there were machines to run them on.

The first computer programmers were women.

What do careers in technology look like today?

People involved in producing software tend to follow three paths:

Design

Product Management

Development

There are an estimated 21 million software developers in the world!

In the United States, they have an average salary around $75,000.

92% of software developers are men.

The previous two facts are related.

Wages in a field tend to rise when in proportion to male representation in that field.

Less than 10% of software developers are black or Latin@.

So I'm not going to tell you that you need to be in this industry.

But this industry needs you.

So what can you do?

Take as much math and science as humanly possible.

Study coding online.

Accept that it's hard.

Never give up!
